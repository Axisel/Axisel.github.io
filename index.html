Yggdrasil Defender

Yggdrasil Defender is a 2D tower defense / platform game that I made with 3 friend over my second semester. This game was made on unity using C#.

In this game you have two phases, in each level you will have to do an exploration phase or you will simply move through the level with monsters trying to stop you. Then, you will do a tower defense phase, you will have to use your weapons and turrets to defend an objective from waves of monsters.
